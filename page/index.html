Algorithm Lists

Explore the world of algorithms and data structures. Each topic is carefully crafted to enhance your understanding, from foundational concepts to advanced techniques.

Sorting Algorithms

Dive into the mechanics of sorting algorithms, including Bubble Sort, Quick Sort, Merge Sort, and Heap Sort. Learn their time complexities, practical use cases, and how they optimize data organization.

Searching Algorithms

Uncover the strategies behind searching algorithms like Binary Search and Linear Search. Master their applications in finding data effectively within vast datasets. Learn how to optimize your search processes to handle real-world challenges in data retrieval and analysis.

Data Structures

Understand foundational data structures like Arrays, Stacks, Queues, Linked Lists, and Trees. Learn how they enable efficient data manipulation and storage in real-world scenarios.

Two Pointer Algorithm

Master the Two Pointer technique, a powerful approach to solving array and string problems. Understand its applications in tasks like finding pairs, merging arrays, and solving sliding window problems.

Sliding Window

Master the Sliding Window technique, an essential algorithmic approach for solving problems that involve contiguous subarrays or substrings. Learn how to optimize space and time complexities by dynamically adjusting window boundaries. Common applications include solving problems like maximum sum subarray, finding the longest substring, and more.

Backtracking

Explore Backtracking, a powerful algorithmic technique for solving complex problems like generating permutations, solving mazes, and the N-Queens problem. Understand how to traverse decision trees by exploring all potential solutions, while efficiently pruning paths to optimize performance.
