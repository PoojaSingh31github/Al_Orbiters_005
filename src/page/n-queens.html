N-Queens Problem

The N-Queens problem involves placing N chess queens on an NÃ—N chessboard such that no two queens threaten each other. It explores backtracking algorithms to ensure valid placements step by step.

Time Complexity:

O(N!), where N is the size of the chessboard. This accounts for generating permutations and validating placements.

Space Complexity:

O(N), for storing the board and backtracking stack.
